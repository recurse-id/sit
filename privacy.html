Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Sit is a meditation timer. It collects no data about you.

What we collect

Nothing. Sit has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs that collect personal information. All settings are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

Data stored on your device

Your timer settings (duration, bells, volume, haptics) are saved in your device's local storage. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-party services

Sit uses no third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Children's privacy

Sit does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
