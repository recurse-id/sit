FAQ

No sound is playing
Make sure your device isn't in silent mode, or switch the bell output to "Haptic only" in the bells screen. You can also adjust volume in Settings.
How do I pause a session?
Tap anywhere on the active timer screen to show the controls, then tap Pause. Tap Resume to continue.
Can I set an interval bell?
Yes — in the Bells screen, you can add interval bells that ring at regular intervals throughout your session.
Where is my data stored?
All settings are stored locally on your device. Sit has no accounts and no servers.
